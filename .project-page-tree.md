## 4. The Reading Engine (Core Functionality)
*Goal: The specialized speed-reading experience.*

- **4.1. Reading Overlay:** On-the-fly controls for WPM speed and mode switching.
- **4.2. RSVP Mode:** Single-word stream with **ORP (Optimal Recognition Point)** highlighting.
- **4.3. Bionic Mode:** Full-text block with bolded initial fixations.
- **4.4. Chunking Mode:** Animated groups of 2-3 words appearing simultaneously.
- **4.5. Guided Scrolling:** Neon "Pacer" line moving under the text block.
- **4.6. AI Bridge (Visualization):** - Full-screen modal triggered every 300 words.
    - SiliconFlow (Flux.1-Schnell) API integration for real-time imagery.
    - Social sharing and "Save to Story Gallery" options.

---

## 5. Training Lab (Gamification)
*Goal: Eye muscle and focus exercises.*

- **5.1. Warm-up Drills:**
    - **5.1.1. Schulte Table:** 5x5 interactive grid for peripheral number searching.
    - **5.1.2. Eye-Stretch:** Guiding focus to extreme corners of the screen.
- **5.2. Focus Games:**
    - **5.2.1. Peripheral Catch:** Identifying words in the margins without breaking central focus.
    - **5.2.2. Saccadic Jumps:** Rapid eye-movement training between two points.
